iTendr

Corporate and government dining is high value and market-maker iTendr ensures that it's treated as such. iPhone app iTendr enables public and private sector bookers to enter dining details once and quickly receive multiple dining options from appropriate venues. iTendr instantly alerts high calibre dining rooms of your reservation needs allowing them to conﬁrm their availability and suitability. iTendr works for reservations of all size, can be tailored toward local businesses, is free for bookers and completely private. All booking arrangements are executed in normal fashion off-app once a venue is selected by you.
